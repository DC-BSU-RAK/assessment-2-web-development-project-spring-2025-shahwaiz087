Admission Requirements

Green Valley Secondary School welcomes students who are committed to academic excellence and character development. Below are the requirements for different grade levels:

Grade 6 Admission

Age: 10-11 years

Previous Grade: Completion of Grade 5

Minimum Marks: 60% in Grade 5

Grade 7-8 Admission

Age: 11-13 years

Previous Grade: Completion of previous grade

Minimum Marks: 65% in previous grade

Grade 9-10 Admission

Age: 13-16 years

Previous Grade: Completion of Grade 8/9

Minimum Marks: 70% in previous grade

Note: All admissions are subject to entrance test and interview. Meeting minimum requirements does not guarantee admission.

Required Documents

Please bring the following documents at the time of application submission:

Essential Documents:

  • Completed application form (available at school office)
  • Original and photocopy of student's Birth Certificate/B-Form
  • Original and photocopy of previous academic records/report cards
  • Character certificate from previous school (if applicable)
  • Transfer certificate from previous school (if applicable)
  • Father's/Guardian's CNIC copy
  • Recent passport-size photographs (4 copies)
  • Medical certificate from registered doctor
  • Vaccination record

Important: Incomplete applications will not be processed. Please ensure all documents are complete and properly attested.

Admission Process

Our admission process is designed to be fair and transparent. Follow these simple steps:

1

Application

Submit completed application form with required documents

2

Entrance Test

Student takes written test in core subjects

3

Interview

Student and parent interview with admission committee

4

Result

Admission decision communicated within 3 working days

5

Enrollment

Pay fees and complete enrollment formalities

Entrance Test Syllabus

The entrance test will be based on the curriculum of the previous grade. Test duration is 2 hours.

Test Subjects:

  • English: Grammar, comprehension, and basic writing
  • Mathematics: Basic arithmetic, algebra (for higher grades)
  • Urdu: Grammar and comprehension
  • General Knowledge: Basic Pakistan Studies and Islamic Studies
Test Schedule: Entrance tests are conducted every Saturday at 9:00 AM. Please arrive 30 minutes before the test time.
